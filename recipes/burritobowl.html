Burrito Bowl Recipe

Description

A burrito bowl is a dish filled with all the ingredients typically found in a burrito,

including meat, rice, beans, salsa, guacamole, cheese, and grilled vegetables, in a bowl.

The Mexican-inspired dish makes for a customizable, easy-to-assemble weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Cilantro Lime Rice, or cooked white or brown rice
  • 2 cups cooked black beans , or pinto beans
  • 1 recipe Tofu Sofritas
  • 1 recipe Fajita Veggies, or fresh veggies like romaine lettuce, cherry tomatoes, and corn
  • Pico de Gallo, or Tomatillo Salsa, Homemade Salsa, or Corn Salsa
  • Guacamole
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Lime wedges, for serving

Steps

  1. Divide the rice among four bowls. Top with the beans, sofritas, fajita veggies, and scoops of salsa and guacamole.
  2. Garnish with desired toppings and season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve with lime wedges for squeezing.
